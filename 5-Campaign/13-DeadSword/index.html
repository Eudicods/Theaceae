Awaken the Sword¶

The party is back in their home plane. Agnes wants them to help her save the Sisters.

Welcome Home¶

The party returns to Camellia to discover Mayhem just returning home. They will receive an offer from Marquis Drach to assassinate Wahn and Sister Agnes.

Spies¶

Marquis Drach will leave assassins to monitor the party while they decide if they will accept his offer. He will return in 2 days time to see what the party says.

Sneak Off¶

The party has decided to attempt to sneak off before Marquis Drach returns so they don’t have to answer demands.

Domov Again¶

The party needs to get inside of Domov and determine where Sister Agnes is located.

Grace’s Story¶

Sister Agnes reveals her big plan for saving the Sisters and driving the Illithid Empire out of this realm. She wants to reunite the sword, Grace, with the soul of the founder of the Sisters and block all inter-dimensional travel to or from this realm.

Grace’s Tomb¶

The party travels to Grace’s tomb and starts investigating. All they know is that they need to find the key to Grace’s burial chamber.

Finishing the Tomb¶

Returning to Domov¶

Having finished the quest to reunite Grace’s soul with the sword, the party has the chance to resupply in Domov.

He Who Dreams¶

He Who Dreams needs the party’s help to resolve an issue. The river running by the forest has decreased significantly and the druids sent to investigate have not come back yet.
