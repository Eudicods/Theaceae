East Side¶

The east side of the White Spine mountains is much more fertile, but the thick jungles have many dangers, such as gnolls and jungle creatures. Most people have banded together into a pair of cities. The capital city Camellia is the largest city in the region and the home of huge tea plantations that provide the region’s chief export. Duro is the port city that acts at the gateway between Theaceae and the rest of the world.
